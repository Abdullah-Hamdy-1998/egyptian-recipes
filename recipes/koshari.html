Egyptian Koshari

Description

Many mothers are looking for recipes to make Egyptian koshari at home without exorbitant costs, to dispense with the habit of buying it from shops and restaurants.

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 300g vermicelli
  • 300g small ring pasta
  • 3 cups of washed Egyptian rice
  • 3 cups of black lentils
  • 2 tablespoons of cumin
  • Salt
  • 500g of soaked and half-cooked chickpeas
  • 2 quartered onions
  • 2 quartered tomatoes
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 hot green pepper
  • 1 hot red pepper
  • 1 lemon slices
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin
  • 2 teaspoons of dried coriander
  • Salt
  • Water
  • 6 Julian white onions
  • Flour
  • Plenty of oil for frying
  • 1 liter of tomato juice
  • 8 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of cumin
  • 2 teaspoons of dried coriander
  • A dash of nutmeg
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons of sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of hot harissa
  • Water
  • Salt
  • 6 cloves of finely minced garlic
  • Half a cup of vinegar
  • Half a cup of lemon juice
  • Chickpeas boiling water
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of dried coriander
  • Salt

How to prepare

To make onion frying:

Stir the onion well in the flour and remove any excess flour on the onion and fry it in very hot oil.

To make chickpeas:

We first half-cook the chickpeas. Then add the onion, pepper, garlic, tomatoes, coriander, paprika, and cumin and add boiling water to it, cover the chickpeas with boiling water, cover and leave it to cook.

To make rice with lentils:

We first half-cook the lentils alone. Then we take from the onion frying oil and put it in the cooking pot and put the rice on it and stir and add salt and hot water the same amount as the rice and stir and before it completes its cooking we add the half-cooked lentils and cumin and a little onion frying oil and stir and cover it and leave it to complete its cooking.

To make vermicelli:

We first fry it in onion frying oil then we put boiling water on it and leave it to cook on a low heat.

To make koshari sauce:

In a pot on the fire, we put onion frying oil and put minced garlic on it and dried coriander and bay leaves and cumin and after it acquires a golden color we put tomato juice on it and salt and black pepper and sugar that equals the acidity of the tomatoes and stir and leave it to simmer.

To make koshari hot sauce:

In a pot on the fire, we put oil from frying the onion and put harissa hot and leave it to boil well and then we add water with quick stirring and leave it to boil and condense.

To make koshari vinegar:

In a bowl, we put garlic and lemon juice and vinegar and cumin and dried coriander and salt and stir well and dilute it with a little chickpeas boiling water and stir and with that, we have prepared the vinegar with us.

To assemble the dish:

We put the rice and on it the small pasta and the spaghetti after putting a little oil on it to separate it from each other after boiling and on the face we put another rice with lentils and put the sauce on the face and on it the fried onion and chickpeas on it.
